Sochi Beach

There is no other place in the world you get to enjoy the black sea as here! Construction wise it's wide, long with alot of options for scenery. The stones are peculiar yet astonishing, variety of food spots, bicycle parking and rentals, the breeze is super silent sometimes very calm and peaceful, many hotels, restaurants to choose from including chains like McDonalds and KFC, it stretches from adler town to Rusky Dom areas, it's easily Accessible and safe, with parks friendly locals with exciting activities of the Russian culture from pet monkeys, tigers to solo beach performances that make this place worthwhile, the sand is dark, acess to sun and swimming is for everyone with tent rentals and public changing rooms.
